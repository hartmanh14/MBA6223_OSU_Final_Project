# LSP — Longshot Stock Prediction

A daily stock analysis dashboard that delivers a **Buy / Hold / Sell** signal
for any S&P 500 company, powered by early market data, financial fundamentals,
and Google Trends macro sentiment.

Live demo: [Deployed on Render.com](https://lsp-stock-prediction.onrender.com)

---

## What the app does

Every trading day at **9:40 AM ET** (10 minutes after market open), the app:

1. Downloads the first 10 minutes of 1-minute intraday bars for all ~500 S&P 500 stocks
2. Runs 5 technical/macro indicators per stock to produce a score in [-5, +5]
3. Assigns a **BUY** (score ≥ +2), **HOLD**, or **SELL** (score ≤ -2) signal
4. Caches results — the rest of the day users get fast, sub-100ms responses

Users can then:
- Search any S&P 500 ticker or company name
- See the signal prominently displayed alongside 10 key financial metrics
- View a live Google Trends panel tracking 10 macro search terms

---

## Signal Logic

Five indicators each vote **+1** (bullish), **0** (neutral), or **−1** (bearish).

| # | Indicator | Bullish (+1) | Bearish (−1) |
|---|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Gap** | Open ≥ +1% vs prev close | Open ≤ −1% |
| 2 | **Momentum** | 10-min return ≥ +0.3% | ≤ −0.3% |
| 3 | **VWAP** | Last price ≥ VWAP +0.1% | ≤ VWAP −0.1% |
| 4 | **Volume** | Vol ratio ≥ 1.5× (confirms momentum) | — |
| 5 | **Macro Trend** | Google bullish terms outweigh bearish | Bearish terms dominate |

**Score ≥ +2 → BUY · Score ≤ −2 → SELL · Otherwise → HOLD**

## How the daily refresh works

Two complementary mechanisms fire the morning refresh:

### 1. APScheduler (in-process)
`app.py` starts a `BackgroundScheduler` that fires `_daily_refresh()` at
exactly **9:40 AM ET, Monday–Friday**.  This works as long as the Render dyno
is awake when the trigger fires.

### 2. External cron via `/api/refresh` (recommended on free tier)
Render's free tier spins down idle dynos after 15 minutes.  To guarantee the
refresh fires even after a cold start, set up a free cron job at
[cron-job.org](https://cron-job.org) to POST to your app at 9:42 AM ET:

```
URL:     https://lsp-stock-prediction.onrender.com/api/refresh
Method:  POST
Header:  X-Refresh-Secret: <your REFRESH_SECRET value>
Cron:    42 13 * * 1-5     (13:42 UTC = 9:42 AM EDT)
         42 14 * * 1-5     (14:42 UTC = 9:42 AM EST)
```

> Run both cron schedules to handle Daylight Saving Time automatically.

### 3. Lazy auto-refresh
As a final fallback, when any user request arrives after 9:40 AM ET on a day
with no cached data, the app triggers a refresh automatically in a background
thread.

## How to trigger a manual refresh

Send a POST request with your refresh secret:

```bash
curl -X POST https://lsp-stock-prediction.onrender.com/api/refresh \
     -H "X-Refresh-Secret: YOUR_SECRET_HERE"
```

The refresh runs asynchronously — the response is immediate but the data
takes a few minutes to compute and cache.

## Deployment on Render (free tier)

1. Push this repo to GitHub (`main` branch)
2. Go to [Render Dashboard](https://dashboard.render.com) → **New** → **Blueprint**
3. Connect your GitHub repo — Render reads `render.yaml` automatically
4. Render generates a `REFRESH_SECRET` automatically; copy it for your cron job
5. Your app will be live at `https://lsp-stock-prediction.onrender.com`

Auto-deploys are enabled — every `git push` to `main` triggers a new deploy.
